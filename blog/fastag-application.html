How to Get FASTag - Apply Online & Activate NHAI FASTag

July 20, 2024 Digital Seva Center Aurangabad

FASTag is an electronic toll collection system that uses RFID technology to deduct toll charges directly from a linked bank account or prepaid wallet as you pass through toll plazas. It is mandatory for all vehicles in India.

Why FASTag is Mandatory

Since February 2021, FASTag is mandatory for all four-wheelers in India. Vehicles without FASTag at toll plazas must pay double the toll amount. FASTag reduces waiting time at tolls and enables cashless toll payment.

How to Get FASTag

  • Bank Website/App: HDFC, SBI, ICICI, Axis, Paytm Bank, etc.
  • NHAI Portal: fastag.nhai.org.in
  • IHMCL Website: ihmcl.com
  • Point of Sale (POS): At select petrol pumps, toll plazas, bank branches
  • Amazon / Flipkart: Purchase FASTag kit online

Documents Required for FASTag KYC

  • Vehicle Registration Certificate (RC)
  • Owner's Aadhaar Card or PAN Card
  • Passport size photograph
  • Vehicle photo (front with number plate visible)
  • Mobile number (linked to bank account)

How to Apply Online

  1. Visit your bank's website or FASTag application page
  2. Fill vehicle details: registration number, vehicle class, chassis number
  3. Complete KYC: upload Aadhaar, RC, photograph
  4. Pay initial balance (minimum Rs. 100) + tag cost (Rs. 100 approx.)
  5. FASTag delivered to your address within 7 days
  6. Affix FASTag on windscreen as per instructions
  7. Recharge when balance is low via bank app or Paytm

FASTag Recharge

You can recharge FASTag through net banking, UPI, debit/credit card on your bank app, Paytm, or NHAI portal. Set up auto-recharge to never run out of balance at a toll booth.
