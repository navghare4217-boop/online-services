How to Apply Driving Licence Online in Maharashtra 2024

July 28, 2024 Digital Seva Center Aurangabad

Getting a Driving Licence (DL) in Maharashtra is now easier with the online Sarathi portal. You can apply for learner's licence, book driving test slot, and get your permanent driving licence - all online.

Types of Driving Licences

  • Learner's Licence (LL): For learning to drive (valid 6 months)
  • Permanent Driving Licence (DL): After passing driving test
  • Commercial Driving Licence: For auto, taxi, truck, bus
  • International Driving Permit: For driving abroad

Documents Required

  • Age Proof: Aadhaar card, birth certificate, passport
  • Address Proof: Aadhaar card, utility bill, passport
  • Passport size photographs (6)
  • Medical fitness certificate (Form 1-A) - for commercial vehicles

Step 1: Apply for Learner's Licence

  1. Visit sarathi.parivahan.gov.in
  2. Select Maharashtra as your state
  3. Click "Apply for Learner Licence"
  4. Fill application form with personal details
  5. Upload documents and photograph
  6. Book slot for LL test at RTO
  7. Pay fees (Rs. 200 approx.)
  8. Appear for computerized LL test at RTO
  9. Download LL on same day if passed

Step 2: Apply for Permanent Driving Licence

  1. After 30 days of Learner's Licence, apply for DL test
  2. Visit Sarathi portal, login and apply for DL
  3. Book driving test slot at RTO
  4. Appear with your vehicle for practical test
  5. If passed, DL sent to your address via post (Smart Card DL)

Driving Licence Fees in Maharashtra

  • Learner's Licence: Rs. 200 per vehicle class
  • Permanent DL: Rs. 400 per vehicle class
  • DL Renewal: Rs. 200
  • Duplicate DL: Rs. 400
