Ration Card Maharashtra 2025 – Online Apply, Documents और Types

Ration Card Maharashtra government द्वारा issued एक important document है जो Public Distribution System (PDS) के तहत subsidized food grains और other essential items provide करता है। यह income और family size के basis पर issue होता है।

Maharashtra Ration Card के Types

Card TypeColorIncome LimitBenefits
Antyodaya (AAY)YellowPoorest families35 kg grain/month free
Below Poverty Line (BPL)Yellow/OrangeBPL criteriaSubsidized grain
Above Poverty Line (APL)Saffron/OrangeAll othersMarket rate grain
Non-Priority (White)WhiteHigher incomeIdentity purpose

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card (सभी family members का)
  • Residence proof (electricity bill, rent agreement)
  • Income certificate (BPL/APL के लिए)
  • Family photo
  • Mobile number
  • Previous ration card (यदि था)
  • Gas connection details

Online Apply Process (Mahafood Portal)

1rcms.mahafood.gov.in खोलें — Mahafood portal।
2"New Ration Card" select करें
3Aadhaar verify करें — Mobile OTP से।
4Family details भरें — सभी members का Aadhaar।
5Documents upload करें
6Application submit करें — Reference number save करें।
7Field verification — Officer घर verify करने आएगा।
8Card मिलेगा — 30-60 days में।

Ration Card में Changes कैसे करें?

  • Member add करना: Birth certificate + Aadhaar
  • Member delete करना: Death certificate
  • Address change: New address proof
  • Name correction: Affidavit + supporting document
