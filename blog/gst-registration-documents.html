Documents Required for GST Registration in India 2024

November 8, 2024 Digital Seva Center Aurangabad

GST (Goods and Services Tax) registration is mandatory for businesses with annual turnover exceeding Rs. 20 lakhs (Rs. 10 lakhs for special category states). It gives your business a unique GSTIN number.

Who Needs to Register for GST?

  • Businesses with turnover above Rs. 20 lakhs per year
  • E-commerce sellers (no threshold limit)
  • Businesses making inter-state supplies
  • Casual taxable persons
  • Non-resident taxable persons

Documents for Sole Proprietorship / Individual

  • PAN Card of the proprietor
  • Aadhaar Card of the proprietor
  • Photograph of the proprietor
  • Bank account details (cancelled cheque or bank statement)
  • Proof of business address (electricity bill, rent agreement)

Documents for Partnership Firm

  • PAN Card of the partnership firm
  • Partnership deed
  • PAN and Aadhaar of all partners
  • Photographs of all partners
  • Bank account details of firm
  • Proof of place of business

Documents for Private Limited / LLP Company

  • PAN Card of the company
  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • Memorandum of Association (MOA) and Articles of Association (AOA)
  • PAN and Aadhaar of all directors
  • Board resolution / authorized signatory letter
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) of authorized signatory
  • Bank account details
  • Proof of registered office address

GST Registration Process

  1. Visit GST portal: gst.gov.in
  2. Click on "Register Now" under "Taxpayers"
  3. Fill Part A: Business details, PAN, email, mobile
  4. Verify OTP received on email and mobile
  5. Note the TRN (Temporary Reference Number)
  6. Fill Part B with complete business details and upload documents
  7. Verify using DSC or Aadhaar OTP
  8. Submit and track application status

GST Registration Fees and Timeline

GST registration is completely free of cost on the government portal. The registration certificate (GSTIN) is issued within 3-7 working days after document verification.
