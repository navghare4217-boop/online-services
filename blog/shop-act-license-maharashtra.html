Shop Act License Maharashtra 2025 – Online Apply, Documents और Fees

Maharashtra Shops and Establishments Act (1948) के तहत Maharashtra में हर shop, commercial establishment, hotel, restaurant, amusement center को Shop Act License (Gumasta License) लेना mandatory है। यह license Labour Department / Municipal Corporation द्वारा issued होता है।

Shop Act License किसे चाहिए?

  • Retail shops (grocery, medical, clothing)
  • Restaurants और food establishments
  • Hotels और lodges
  • Salons और beauty parlors
  • Offices और commercial spaces
  • Factories (small scale)
  • Any business with employees

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card (owner का)
  • PAN Card
  • Shop address proof (rent agreement या ownership document)
  • Passport size photograph
  • NOC from landlord (यदि rented)
  • GST certificate (यदि applicable)

Fees Structure (Aurangabad AMC)

EmployeesAnnual Fees
0 employees (self)₹100-300
1-9 employees₹300-500
10-19 employees₹500-1000
20+ employees₹1000+

Online Apply Process

1aaplesarkar.mahaonline.gov.in खोलें — Maharashtra government portal।
2Register/Login करें — New user registration।
3"Shops and Establishment" select करें — Labour Department section में।
4Form भरें — Business name, address, type, employees count।
5Documents upload करें — सभी required documents।
6Fees pay करें — Online payment।
7License receive करें — 7-15 days में।

Renewal कब करें?

Shop Act License annual renewal होती है। December 31 से पहले renewal जरूरी है। Late renewal पर penalty लगती है।

⚠️ Penalty: बिना Shop Act License के business चलाने पर ₹2,000-10,000 fine और license suspend हो सकता है।
