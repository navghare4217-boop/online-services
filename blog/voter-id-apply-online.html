How to Apply Voter ID Card Online - New Registration & Correction

September 8, 2024 Digital Seva Center Aurangabad

The Voter Identity Card (EPIC) is an important identity document issued by the Election Commission of India. It is mandatory for voting in elections and serves as valid identity proof across India.

Who Can Apply for Voter ID?

  • Indian citizens who are 18 years or older
  • Ordinary resident of the constituency
  • Not disqualified under any law

New Voter Registration (Form 6)

  1. Visit voters.eci.gov.in or download Voter Helpline App
  2. Click "Register as New Voter" (Form 6)
  3. Enter personal details: name, DOB, address
  4. Upload documents: age proof, address proof, passport photo
  5. Submit application online
  6. Track status using reference number
  7. Voter ID card delivered by post or collect from BLO

Voter ID Correction (Form 8)

If you need to correct name, address, or other details in your existing Voter ID, use Form 8 on the same portal. Upload supporting documents for the correction requested.

Documents Required

  • Age Proof: Birth certificate, 10th certificate, PAN card, Passport, Aadhaar
  • Address Proof: Electricity bill, Bank passbook, Ration card, Aadhaar
  • Passport size photograph

Check Your Name in Voter List

You can check if your name is in the Electoral Roll at electoralsearch.in. Enter your name, state and district to search. You can also check by EPIC number.
