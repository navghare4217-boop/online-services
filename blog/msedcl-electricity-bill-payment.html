MSEDCL Electricity Bill Payment Online - Complete Guide

August 3, 2024 Digital Seva Center Aurangabad

MSEDCL (Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited), also known as Mahavitaran or MAHADISCOM, supplies electricity to most of Maharashtra. Paying your electricity bill online is quick and easy. Here's everything you need to know.

Ways to Pay MSEDCL Bill Online

  • MSEDCL Website: mahadiscom.in
  • MSEDCL App: Mahavitaran mobile app
  • Paytm / PhonePe / GPay: Under "Electricity" section
  • BHIM UPI: Direct payment via UPI
  • Net Banking: Through your bank website
  • Debit/Credit Card: On MSEDCL portal
  • Digital Seva Centers: Like our center in Aurangabad

How to Pay on MSEDCL Website

  1. Go to mahadiscom.in
  2. Click "Pay Bill" on homepage
  3. Enter your CA Number (Consumer Account Number from your bill)
  4. View your outstanding bill amount
  5. Click "Pay Now" and choose payment method
  6. Complete payment and download receipt

How to Find Your CA Number

Your Consumer Account (CA) Number is an 11 to 12 digit number printed on your electricity bill. It is also called Consumer Number or Account Number. Keep this handy for all online transactions.

MSEDCL Bill Not Received - What to Do?

  • Check previous bill or electric meter for CA number
  • Visit MSEDCL website and register for e-bill
  • Visit nearest MSEDCL sub-division office
  • Contact MSEDCL helpline: 19122
