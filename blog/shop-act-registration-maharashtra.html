Shop Act Registration Maharashtra - Complete Online Process

October 20, 2024 Digital Seva Center Aurangabad

The Maharashtra Shops and Establishments Act, 1948 requires all commercial establishments, offices, shops, restaurants, and businesses to register under this act. This registration (also called Gumasta License) is mandatory for all businesses in Maharashtra.

Who Needs Shop Act Registration?

  • All retail and wholesale shops
  • Offices (commercial, industrial, banking)
  • Hotels, restaurants, cafes and eating houses
  • Theaters, cinemas and places of amusement
  • Any establishment employing commercial workers
  • Home-based businesses and freelancers

Benefits of Shop Act Registration

  • Legally recognized business identity
  • Required for opening current bank account
  • Required for GST registration
  • Helps in getting business loans
  • Proof of business existence for various government benefits
  • Required for MSME and other registrations

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card of proprietor/partners
  • PAN Card of proprietor/firm
  • Proof of business address (rent agreement or ownership proof)
  • Electricity bill of business premises
  • Photograph of proprietor
  • Details of employees (number and type)

How to Register Online

  1. Visit Maharashtra Labour Department portal: mahasewa.in or aaplesarkar.mahaonline.gov.in
  2. Register/Login on the portal
  3. Select "Shops and Establishments Registration"
  4. Fill in employer details, establishment details
  5. Enter employee details and working hours
  6. Upload required documents
  7. Pay the registration fee based on number of employees
  8. Submit application and download acknowledgment
  9. Certificate is issued within 3-7 working days

Shop Act Registration Fees in Maharashtra

  • 0 employees: Rs. 500
  • 1-10 employees: Rs. 750
  • 11-20 employees: Rs. 1,000
  • Above 20 employees: Rs. 1,500+
