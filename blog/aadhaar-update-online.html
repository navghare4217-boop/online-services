How to Update Aadhaar Card Online - Name, Address, Mobile

October 5, 2024 Digital Seva Center Aurangabad

Aadhaar Card is India's biometric identity system with 12-digit unique numbers. Keeping your Aadhaar details updated is important for accessing government services, banking, and more. Here's how to update your Aadhaar details online.

What Can You Update in Aadhaar?

  • Name (correction or spelling mistake)
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Language preference

How to Update Address Online (SSUP Portal)

  1. Visit ssup.uidai.gov.in (Self Service Update Portal)
  2. Enter your Aadhaar number
  3. Request OTP on registered mobile number
  4. Login with OTP and select "Address Update"
  5. Enter new address carefully
  6. Upload address proof document (electricity bill, bank passbook, etc.)
  7. Submit and note the URN (Update Request Number)
  8. Updated Aadhaar is mailed/available for download within 7-30 days

How to Update Mobile Number (Aadhaar Enrollment Centre)

Mobile number update in Aadhaar can only be done at an authorized Aadhaar Enrollment Center or Aadhaar Seva Kendra. You need to visit in person with your Aadhaar card and fill a correction form. Biometric verification is done.

Aadhaar Update Fee

  • Online address update: Rs. 50 per update
  • Offline update at enrollment center: Rs. 50
  • Child enrollment: Free

Documents for Aadhaar Address Update

  • Electricity Bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Bank Passbook or Bank Statement
  • Voter ID Card
  • Driving License
  • Passport
  • Government issued address proof

Download Updated e-Aadhaar

After your update is processed, you can download the updated e-Aadhaar from myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in. The e-Aadhaar is equally valid as physical Aadhaar for all purposes.
