Company Registration India में कैसे करें? Types & Process 2024

अपना business legally register करने से आपको legal protection, easy funding और business credibility मिलती है। India में कई types की company registration होती है।

Company Types

  • Sole Proprietorship: Simple, एक person, no registration required
  • Partnership Firm: 2+ partners, Partnership Deed
  • LLP (Limited Liability Partnership): Partners की liability limited
  • OPC (One Person Company): Single founder, corporate benefits
  • Private Limited Company: Most popular, scalable, investor-friendly

Pvt. Ltd. Registration Documents

  • Directors' Aadhaar & PAN
  • Registered office proof
  • DSC of all directors
  • MOA & AOA drafting

Timeline & Cost

3-7 working days | Government fees: ₹5000-₹15000 + professional charges

💡 Company registration से लेकर post-registration compliances तक हम help करते हैं। Contact करें

Company Registration क्यों करें?

Company registration से आपका business एक legal entity बन जाती है। Personal और business assets अलग हो जाते हैं, bank loans आसान होते हैं, और government contracts के लिए eligible होते हैं।

Private Limited Company Registration

Required Documents:

  • All directors का PAN Card और Aadhaar
  • Address proof (electricity bill, bank statement)
  • Passport size photos
  • Registered office proof (rent agreement + NOC)
  • DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) for each director
  • DIN (Director Identification Number)

Process: MCA portal (mca.gov.in) → SPICe+ form → Name reservation → MOA/AOA drafting → Filing → Certificate of Incorporation। 7-15 working days में COI मिलता है।
